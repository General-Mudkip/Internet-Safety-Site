What?

This project was developed as a submission for the BT Young Scientists competition as a way to raise awareness on internet safety.

Why?

We felt that there was a general lack of resources for people, especially for those with limited technical knowledge, to gain an understanding of what they are at risk of online.

Who?

We are a group of 3 TY students at CCC who have rather extensive knowledge on the topic.
